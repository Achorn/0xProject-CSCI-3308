About this Site

Welcome to our Software Development final project! Over the course of the semester, our group has been hard at work to create this website for you! To get started, create an account by inputting your email and a password. After your account has been created, you'll be able to save a list of ingredients you have on hand. That list will help us to generate some tasty and creative suggestions so that you can put those ingredients to good use! Hope you enjoy!
